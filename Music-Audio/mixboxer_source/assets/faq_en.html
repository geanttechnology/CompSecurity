1. How do I switch the video to full screen?
You can rotate your device to landscape for full screen video!

2. Why are some videos missing vocals?
You can go to Settings tab, tick “HD Video & Audio”, and it should be back to normal.

3. Why has the video & audio quality degraded?
You can go to Settings tab, tick “HD Video & Audio”, and your video & audio quality will be improved.

4. I want to add songs to my playlist.
1) Go to Playlists tab, tap and search for songs you like
2) Tap to add to your playlist

Or

1) If you find a song you like from other playlists
2) Tap to the right of the song
3) Tap “Add to another playlist” and then tap “OK” to add the song to your playlist

5. I want to delete songs from my playlist.
1) Tap the playlist you want to edit
2) Tap to the right of the song
3) Tap “Delete” to delete the song

6. I want to create a new playlist
1) Go to Playlist tab, tap to the upper right of the page
2) Enter the name of the new playlist
3) Tap “OK” to create a new playlist

7. I want to delete a playlist
1) Tap the playlist you want to edit
2) Tap to the upper right of the page
3) Tap “Delete” and then “OK” to delete the playlist

8. I want to add/remove a playlist to/from my subscriptions
1) Tap the playlist you want to edit
2) Tap to the upper right of the page, and tap “Like” to add a playlist to my subscriptions, or tap “Unlike” to remove a playlist from my subscriptions.

9. Why do my videos load slowly?
If you’re using 3G, please switch to WIFI or move to a location with better 3G connection and see if it improves.

If you cancel ticking “HD Video and Audio” in Settings tab, it will also help!

10. Can I use MixerBox without Internet?
According to YouTube's guideline, videos must be streamed directly from YouTube, so Internet connection is required to play videos. Therefore, MixBox does NOT support downloading YouTube content for offline listening.

11. How do I adjust the volume?
You can use the volume toggle of your device to adjust the volume.

12. I can’t login with Facebook
If you see the window below, please update your Facebook app.

13. Will MixerBox post my listening activity to my Facebook?
MixerBox will not post your activity to Facebook unless you authorize it. If you login with your Facebook account and would like to automatically share your activities on Facebook, you can tick “Post activities to Facebook” in the Settings tab. You can also hide your activities by canceling ticking “Post activities to Facebook”.

14. Why can’t I find some songs?
Please go to Playlists tab, tap , search for songs you like, and tap “Others”. It will show search results besides videos that are in music category.
However, videos that are restricted from playing on mobile devices by their owners or videos from VEVO will not show in search results.

15. Why is my MixerBox not playing, not responding, or Facebook login window not opening?
1) Please check whether your Internet connection is still working
2) If your Internet connection is normal, please try to restart the app or kill the app.

Please follow these steps:
  • Restart the app:
    Tap X to the upper left of the video to turn off the player. And then tap Back button to exit the app and then relaunch.
  • Kill the app:
    Tap and hold on the Home button (Or double-click the Home button” to kill the app (slide or tap & hold) and then relaunch.
* If your Android OS is 4.1.1, we recommend you to update to 4.1.2 or above for stabler video playing.
  • How to check your OS version?
    Please go to “Settings” of your device, then “About device”, and “Android version” to see your OS version.
  • How to update your OS?
    Please go to “Settings” of your device, then “About device”, and “Software update” to update your OS.

If you have further questions or suggestions, please leave a message at MixerBox Fan Page or send an email to support@mixerbox.com. Please also mention you Android device as well as your Android version. We will get back to you as soon as possible!
